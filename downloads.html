Packages

Core

Basic

Everything from Core, Digital Wellbeing, YouTube Vanced

Omni

Everything from Basic, Google Messages (Replaces AOSP Messages), Google Dialer, Google Contacts (Replaces AOSP Contacts)

Stock

Everything from omni, Google Setup Wizard, Lawnchair (available only on pie package), Google Drive, GBoard, Google Velvet (for voice unlock), Google Markup, Google Photos, Google Clock, Google Sounds, Google Maps, Google Wallpaper, Google Sound Recorder

Full

Everything from Stock, Gmail, Google Play Books, Google Keep, Pixel 4 Live Wallpapers, Google Chrome, Google Assistant
